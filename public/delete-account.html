Delete Your MUWASIWAKI Account

Request permanent deletion of your account and associated data

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an Email Request

Email us at thadev77@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"

Include Your Information

In your email, please provide:

  • Full name registered in the app
  • Email address used for your account
  • Phone number (if provided during registration)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
Confirmation

We will send you a confirmation email within 48 hours to verify your identity

Deletion Process

Once verified, your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose access to all organization news, your membership status, and profile information.

What Data Gets Deleted

✓ Deleted Immediately:
  • Account credentials (email and password)
  • Personal information (name, phone number)
  • Profile data and preferences
  • Family member information
  • NIDA number (if provided)
  • Subregion/location data

What Data May Be Retained

⏳ Retained for Legal/Operational Purposes:
  • Membership application records: Kept for 90 days for audit purposes, then anonymized
  • Transaction/payment history: Kept for 7 years as required by Tanzanian law
  • News articles you created: Retained but authorship anonymized to "Former Member"
  • Backup copies: Removed within 30 days from backup systems

Deletion Timeline

Day 0: Request received

Day 1-2: Identity verification

Day 3-30: Account deactivated and deletion in progress

Day 30: Permanent deletion completed

Day 31-90: Removal from backup systems
